Funnel Interpretation

Where the journey breaks

Users are reaching high purchase intent, but too many fail to complete checkout. That makes basket-to-purchase performance the most important place to investigate.

  • Traffic is entering the funnel.
  • High-intent activity exists.
  • Final-stage completion is the weakest point.

Recommendations

What the business should do next

  • Audit the mobile checkout flow for usability and payment friction.
  • Retarget basket abandoners because they already show strong purchase intent.
  • Segment the funnel further by landing page, traffic source, and visitor type.
  • Validate any new explanation with reproducible SQL before making causal claims.
